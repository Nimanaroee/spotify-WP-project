**10.2 Track / Release Management (Artists)**
***
A dedicated panel for approved artists to fully manage their published works. In this section, artists can upload their audio files, supporting common formats such as MP3 / WAV / FLAC, and insert lyrics for each track.
Furthermore, the ability to select and upload a cover image for an album or a single track, and specify the release type (Single or Album) is provided. Artists can add metadata such as genre, release year, and the names of collaborating artists.
After publishing, artists have access to detailed statistics regarding the number of listeners, streams, and revenue for each work, and if necessary, they can edit or delete published tracks.
